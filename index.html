About the Firm

Three Decades of Technology Leadership

DEBAILLON, LLC is a boutique technology consulting practice built on over 30 years of practitioner experience spanning every domain of information technology — from physical infrastructure and network architecture to enterprise digital strategy and executive IT leadership.

Unlike large consulting firms where senior partners hand off work to junior associates, DEBAILLON clients receive direct engagement from a seasoned technology executive who has managed infrastructure at scale, led digital transformation programs, and guided IT organizations through complex vendor ecosystems and regulatory environments.

Our model is deliberately focused: fewer engagements, deeper involvement, measurably better outcomes. We are a trusted partner — not a vendor — invested in the long-term success of every client we serve.
