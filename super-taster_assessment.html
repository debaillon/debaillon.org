What is a Super-Taster?

Super-tasters are people with heightened taste sensitivity due to having more taste buds (specifically fungiform papillae) on their tongue. They experience flavors more intensely than average tasters, particularly bitter compounds. About 25% of people are super-tasters, 50% are medium tasters, and 25% are non-tasters.
